About

With 12 Years + Experience

Dr. Abhishek NK Saha is an orthopaedic surgeon who deals primarily with complex and paediatric orthopaedics cases.
His area of expertise includes cerebral palsy, revision joint replacements,revision of infected cases,limb lengthening and complex arthroscopic surgeries.
A pioneer of arthroscopy in the paediatric hip and knee,, Dr. ANK Saha has treated rare disorders like discoid meniscus, avascular necrosis of hip and femoral head deformity.
Dr. Saha also has expertise in joint replacements, arthroscopy/ ACL Surgery,sports injuries and complex hand and foot/ankle problems. He is a firm believer that every orthopaedic problem, however complex it might look, always has a solution..

Education: MBBS(gold medallist), DNB, MNAMS, paediatric orthopaedics fellowship, IOA JMF
