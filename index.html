Dr. Abhishek NK Saha

MBBS(gold medallist), DNB, MNAMS, paediatric orthopaedics fellowship, IOA JMF

Dr. Abhishek NK Saha is a Pediatric/Complex orthopedics surgeon who deals primarily with Complicated cases like Complex/Revision Joint replacement, Congenital deformity and Complex Ligament surgeries in children and adults.

About

With 25 Years + Experience

A brilliant diagnostician for rare disorders, Dr. ANK Saha has diagnosed/treated rare conditions like fracture`s in cerebral palsy,TB of joints and has successfully treated revision cases of joint replacement, ACL tumors and Pediatric Hip AVN. He has been a faculty across national and international conferences in the last few years and was the youngest faculty at the Nagpur ,POSICON. He is also one of Pioneers of Pediatric orthopedics care in this part of the world and is the Secretary of the Paed ortho society of west Bengal at present.

Education: MBBS(gold medallist), DNB, MNAMS, paediatric orthopaedics fellowship, IOA JMF
